0. In the name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Kind.

1. When the sky splits apart.

2. And when the stars scatter.

3. And when the oceans explode and overflow.

4. And when the graves are dug open.

5. Then each soul will know exactly what it has done, and what it has left undone.

6. O human being, what has deceived you about your Generous Lord?

7. Who created you, shaped you, and balanced you perfectly.

8. Forming you in whatever way He wished.

9. No! But you deny the Day of Judgment.

10. Yet there are guardians watching over you.

11. Noble recorders.

12. Who know everything you do.

13. The good people will be in complete happiness.

14. And the wicked people will be in blazing fire.

15. They will enter it on the Day of Judgment.

16. And they will never escape from it.

17. And what will make you realize what the Day of Judgment is?

18. Again, what will make you realize what the Day of Judgment is?

19. It is the Day when no soul can help another soul at all, and on that Day, all authority belongs to Allah.
