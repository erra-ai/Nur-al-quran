0. In the name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Kind.

1. By the morning brightness.

2. And by the night when it grows calm and still.

3. Your Lord has not left you, nor does He dislike you.

4. And surely, the Hereafter will be better for you than the first.

5. Soon your Lord will give you so much, and you will be happy.

6. Didn't He find you an orphan, and then gave you shelter?

7. Didn't He find you lost, and then guided you?

8. Didn't He find you in need, and then made you rich?

9. So never be harsh with the orphan.

10. And never turn away someone who asks for help.

11. And always speak about the blessings your Lord has given you.
