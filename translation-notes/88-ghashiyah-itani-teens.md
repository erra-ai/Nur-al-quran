0. In the name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Kind.

1. Has the news of the overwhelming event reached you?

2. On that day, some faces will be humbled.

3. Working hard and exhausted.

4. Entering into a blazing fire.

5. Given drink from a boiling spring.

6. They will have no food except bitter thorns.

7. Which neither nourish nor ease hunger.

8. But on that day, faces will be joyful.

9. Happy with their efforts.

10. In a high Paradise.

11. Where they won't hear any useless talk.

12. In it, there is a flowing spring.

13. And raised couches to rest on.

14. And cups placed ready.

15. And cushions lined up neatly.

16. And carpets spread out everywhere.

17. Don't they look at the camels — how they were created?

18. And at the sky — how it was raised high?

19. And at the mountains — how they were set firm?

20. And at the earth — how it was spread out?

21. So remind them — you are only a reminder.

22. You have no control over them.

23. But whoever turns away and disbelieves.

24. Allah will punish him with the greatest punishment.

25. Truly, to Us is their return.

26. Then surely, with Us is their judgment.
