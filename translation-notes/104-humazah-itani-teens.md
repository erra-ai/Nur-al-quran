0. In the name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Kind.

1. Suffering awaits every person who mocks and insults others.

2. Who gathers wealth, and keeps counting it.

3. Thinking their money will make them live forever.

4. No! They will surely be thrown into the Crusher.

5. And what will make you understand what the Crusher is?

6. It is Allah's blazing fire.

7. Which rises straight into the hearts.

8. It will close in upon them.

9. With towering columns all around.
