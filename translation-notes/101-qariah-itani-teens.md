0. In the name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Kind.

1. The Striking Disaster!

2. What is the Striking Disaster?

3. And what can make you understand what the Striking Disaster is?

4. It is the Day when people will be like scattered moths.

5. And the mountains will be like fluffy wool.

6. Then, whoever's scales are heavy.

7. They will enjoy a happy life.

8. But whoever's scales are light.

9. Their home will be the deep pit.

10. And what can make you understand what that is?

11. It is a blazing, burning fire!
