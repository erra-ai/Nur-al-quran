0. In the name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Kind.

1. Read in the name of your Lord Who created.

2. Created the human being from a tiny clot.

3. Read, and your Lord is the Most Generous.

4. Who taught by the pen.

5. Taught the human being what he didn't know.

6. But no, the human being truly crosses the limits.

7. Because he sees himself as independent.

8. Yet surely, to your Lord is the return.

9. Have you seen the one who stops.

10. A servant when he prays?

11. Have you thought — what if that servant is following the right way.

12. Or encouraging others to do good?

13. Have you thought — what if that person rejects the truth and turns away?

14. Doesn't he know that Allah sees everything?

15. No! If he doesn't stop, We will surely drag him by the forehead.

16. A lying, sinful forehead.

17. Then let him call his supporters.

18. We will call the guards of Hell.

19. No! Don't obey him. Bow down in prayer, and come closer to Allah.
